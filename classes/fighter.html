Fighter

A versatile martial combatant who masters weapons, tactics, and battlefield control.

Fighters are highly adaptable warriors capable of excelling in a wide range of combat styles. They rely on consistent weapon attacks, strong action economy, and flexible build options to remain effective in nearly any situation.

Overview

  • Primary Role: Carry / Tank
  • Combat Style: Melee or Ranged
  • Complexity: Low
  • Key Abilities: Strength or Dexterity, Constitution

Fighters are defined by their consistency and flexibility. While simple at a basic level, they offer room for deeper optimization through positioning, timing, and build choices.

Core Traits

  • Primary Ability: Strength or Dexterity
  • Hit Point Die: D10 per Fighter level
  • Saving Throw Proficiencies: Strength, Constitution
  • Skill Proficiencies: Choose 2: Acrobatics, Animal Handling, Athletics, History, Insight, Intimidation, Perception, Survival
  • Weapon Proficiencies: Simple Weapons, Martial Weapons
  • Tool Proficiencies: None
  • Armor Training: Light Armor, Medium Armor, Heavy Armor, Shields
  • Starting Equipment: Choose A or B: (A) Chain Mail, Shield, Longsword, Light Crossbow, 20 Bolts, and Explorer’s Pack; or (B) 155 GP

Core Mechanics

Fighting Style

A passive specialization that enhances your preferred combat approach, such as improving defense, weapon damage, or accuracy.

Second Wind

A self-healing ability that restores hit points, allowing you to stay in combat longer without relying on external support.

Action Surge

Grants an additional action on your turn for a limited number of uses. This is one of the Fighter’s most powerful tools, enabling burst damage or rapid tactical responses.

Level 1 Features

  • Fighting Style — Choose a combat specialization
  • Second Wind — Self-healing ability

At level 1, the Fighter establishes a flexible foundation, allowing you to define your preferred combat style from the beginning.

Playstyle

In Combat

  • Deliver consistent weapon-based damage
  • Adapt positioning based on enemy behavior
  • Use Action Surge for key moments or burst turns

Resource Management

  • Action Surge is limited and should be used strategically
  • Second Wind provides reliable self-sustain

Out of Combat

  • Limited utility compared to spellcasters
  • Can contribute through physical skills and presence

Subclasses (Martial Archetypes)

Fighters choose a Martial Archetype that defines their combat specialization.

  • Champion — Focuses on simplicity and improved physical performance
  • Battle Master — Uses maneuvers for tactical control and versatility
  • Eldritch Knight — Combines martial combat with limited spellcasting
  • Psi Warrior — Uses psionic abilities to enhance combat and defense

Each archetype shifts the Fighter’s playstyle toward either simplicity, tactical depth, or hybrid capabilities.

Strengths & Weaknesses

Strengths

  • Highly consistent damage output
  • Flexible build options
  • Strong action economy with Action Surge

Weaknesses

  • Limited utility outside combat
  • Less impactful abilities compared to spellcasters
  • Can feel repetitive without tactical variation

Building a Fighter

Ability Score Priorities

  • Strength or Dexterity — Depends on weapon choice
  • Constitution — Improves durability

General Advice

  • Choose a Fighting Style that matches your intended build
  • Save Action Surge for impactful moments
  • Position carefully to maximize effectiveness
