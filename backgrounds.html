Backgrounds

Backgrounds define your character’s life before adventuring. They provide both narrative context and important mechanical benefits.

Each background grants three ability scores. Increase all three by +1, or increase one by +2 and another by +1.

Backgrounds also provide a feat, skill and tool proficiencies, and starting equipment.

Structured Roles

  • Ability Scores: Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma
  • Feat: Magic Initiate (Cleric)
  • Skill Proficiencies: Insight and Religion
  • Tool Proficiency: Calligrapher’s Supplies
